About Us

This page was created by women, for women, with the goal of providing accurate and accessible information about women’s rights and health. Centered around the St. Edward’s University community, our mission is to raise awareness, foster conversation, and inspire action on campus regarding issues that impact women. We aim to empower students with knowledge and build a supportive space for advocacy and education.
