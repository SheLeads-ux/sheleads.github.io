Student Organization

FLIP

FLIP is a student-run organization that promotes political activism and civic engagement through a feminist lens, focusing on voter education, advocacy, and student-led projects. We empower Women and Gender Non-Conforming Individuals by building leadership skills and organizing events like voter registration drives, policy workshops, and menstruation drives.

It's On Us

It’s On Us is a national organization focusing on educating college campuses about sexual assault and gender violence. The chapter here at St. Edward’s is focused on fostering a community on campus to validate survivors, provide an outlet for sharing their stories, and educate each other on how best to support our St. Edward’s community.

Texas Rising

Texas Rising engages students in progressive issue advocacy and civic participation by promoting voter education, political awareness, and community involvement. In partnership with the Texas Freedom Network, we work to amplify student voices, hold representatives accountable, and empower young people to advocate for social justice and participate in every election.

Women In Tech

BWomen in Technology (WiT) at St. Edward's University fosters an inclusive community for women-identifying students interested in tech by offering mentorship, networking, and professional development opportunities. Through events, workshops, and collaborations, WiT empowers members to pursue leadership roles and helps bridge the gender gap in the technology industry.

Health and Counseling Center

The Health and Counseling Center (HCC) is committed to providing quality services focused on enhancing academic success, personal development and wellbeing. We want to make it as easy as possible for all St. Edward’s University students to get connected to services — especially during this time of uncertainty and concern brought on by the Covid-19 pandemic.

Services We Provide
Located in Lady Bird Johnson Hall, the Health and Counseling Center provides two different types of services: Health Services and Counseling Services.

Health Services
Partnering with Ascension Medical Group Seton (AMG), the HCC provides convenient, affordable, and quality health care including a wide range of acute and preventative services. Students, faculty, and staff may receive care through HCC’s Health Services.
Make a Health Services Appointment

Counseling Services
Serving all enrolled St. Edward’s University students, the HCC’s expert clinical staff provides a variety of individual counseling opportunities, group services, and topical well-being workshops.

Students can call the Hilltopper Helpline 24/7/365 to receive real-time, immediate support.

Our Commitment to LiveWellSEU
LiveWellSEU is a university initiative that centers on a holistic approach to wellness and well-being. LiveWellSEU helps students, faculty and staff prioritize their well-being so that they can thrive in all areas of life — mind, body and spirit. Through LiveWellSEU, St. Edward’s helps you develop skills and life-long coping strategies so that you can learn how to take care of yourself and succeed while on the hilltop — and beyond.

Behavioral Intervention Team
The St. Edward’s Behavioral Intervention Team (BIT) cares for students, faculty and staff who may be in distress. Keep our community healthy and safe — learn how to report a concern, identify the behaviors of someone in distress and become familiar with available resources
