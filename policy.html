The Guttmacher Institute

The Guttmacher Institute, established in 1968, is a leading nonprofit research and policy organization dedicated to advancing sexual and reproductive health and rights worldwide. It conducts comprehensive research, analyzes policies, and provides public education on topics such as contraception, abortion, and maternal health. The Institute's work informs policymakers, health professionals, and the public to promote evidence-based policies and programs that support reproductive health and rights.

NWLC

The National Women's Law Center (NWLC), founded in 1972, is a non-profit organization based in Washington, D.C., dedicated to advocating for gender justice. The NWLC focuses on issues central to the lives of women and girls, including economic security, education, employment, and health, with special attention to the concerns of low-income women. The Center employs various strategies to advance its mission, such as litigating cases of sex discrimination, filing amicus briefs to protect and expand rights, and raising public awareness of legal protections.

Center for Reproductive Rights

The Center for Reproductive Rights is a global legal advocacy organization founded in 1992 that seeks to advance reproductive rights as fundamental human rights. Utilizing the power of law, the Center works to ensure that reproductive freedoms are protected and upheld by governments worldwide. Their efforts include litigating landmark cases, influencing policy, and engaging in advocacy to promote access to reproductive healthcare services such as contraception, safe abortion, and maternal care.....
