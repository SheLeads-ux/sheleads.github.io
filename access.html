Twenty Eight Health

What They Do: TwentyEight Health provides affordable and accessible healthcare to all women — no matter where they are, now or in the future.
How They Help: They offer sexual health education, telehealth services, medication delivery, and on-demand guidance, ensuring that women receive the support they need. Their services are discreet, affordable, and designed to be as convenient as possible.
Achievements: TwentyEight Health has expanded access to vital healthcare for thousands of women, helping them navigate sexual health with ease and confidence. Through their affordable and discreet services, they’ve removed barriers to care, making it easier for women to get the healthcare they deserve, no matter their circumstances.

National Family & Planning Reproductive Health Association

What They Do: They promote the importance of family planning within the nation’s healthcare system while providing accessible information about reproductive care.
How They Help: They connect individuals with high-quality, client-centered, affordable, and comprehensive family planning and sexual health services, offering access to providers of their choice.
Achievements: They have expanded access to family planning services nationwide, ensuring that individuals — regardless of background or income — can receive trusted care and make informed decisions about their reproductive health.

Abortion On Our Terms

What They Do: Abortion On Our Own Terms supports women and individuals in making informed healthcare decisions by providing safe, private options for managing their own abortion when needed.
How They Help: They offer online and in-person informational sessions about self-managed abortion, including education on the safe use of abortion pills and available care options. Their goal is to ensure individuals have access to accurate information, support, and resources.
Achievements: Abortion On Our Own Terms has expanded access to trustworthy, evidence-based information, helping individuals understand their options and make decisions that are right for their health and circumstances.

Jane's Due Process

What They Do: Jane’s Due Process supports young people in Texas by helping them access critical health information, including birth control, abortion care, and family planning services — all with confidential and secure assistance, regardless of their age.
How They Help: They offer a confidential text line connecting young people to trusted information about birth control, abortion access, and family planning — without requiring parental involvement. They also work to uplift and center the voices of young people in conversations about their healthcare and rights.
Achievements: Jane’s Due Process has guided countless young Texans through the challenges of parental consent laws and state abortion restrictions. They provide direct funding and support for those who must travel out of Texas to receive healthcare, and continue working toward a future where all young people can make informed, independent decisions about their bodies and their lives.
